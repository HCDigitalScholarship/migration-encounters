Sergio:        Okay. So Sergio here with Cesar doing the interview. So Cesar, tell me why you and your family went to the United States in the first place.

Cesar:        Yeah.

Cesar:        No, I never grew up in Mexico. My mom was living already in United States when she got pregnant with me. She got in a fight with my dad, as this is what everybody says. And she went back, she was eight months pregnant. She had me and a month and a half later, my dad came back for-

Cesar:        Basically, "Hey, you know what? I'm sorry." I don't know what problems they had but that's what happened. Selling drugs.
